All About Jen

Greetings! My humble beginnings start out in Michigan, where I graduated from Grand Valley State University with a degree in International Relations. Without any job prospects, I moved to Seattle with my boyfriend in 2012 and have been here since. That same year I got a job at The Advocates law firm as the very first Seattle employee. I've learned a lot here, and moved into an IT position for the firm. I took a few programming courses at Oregon State University, intending to actually get a CS degree, but personal life got in the way. I'm still doing IT here at The Advocates and am hoping to soon move into a webmaster position for our Seattle office! Also, I like cats.
